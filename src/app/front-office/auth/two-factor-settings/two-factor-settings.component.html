Enhance the security of your account by enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).

How to Enable 2FA:
  1. Click the "Generate QR Code" button below.
  2. A QR code will appear on the screen.
  3. Open an authenticator app on your phone (e.g., Google Authenticator, Authy).
  4. Use the app to scan the QR code.
  5. After scanning, enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app in the input field.
  6. Click "Enable 2FA" to complete the process.

How to Disable 2FA:

  2. Enter the code in the input field below.
  3. Click "Disable 2FA" to confirm.
